Collaboration

Working with people allows you to get different ideas, split the work and consult each other to solve problems easier. It can be really fun to work with people when everyone is on board. It's an important skill since we'll most likely be working with others in our future careers.

Typically, I work in groups for schools projects when I need to. I listen to what everyone has to say and try to put all our ideas together. Typically, these projects turn out great. I also like to work with my friends to complete personal projects sometimes for fun.

I would really like to be a part of a team or more clubs at school to work more on my collaboration.
